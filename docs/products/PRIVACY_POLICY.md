### 2. Data yang Kami Kumpulkan

#### a. Data Akun

- Kode unik (hash) dari alamat email Google Anda — email asli tidak disimpan
- Nama tampilan yang Anda atur dalam Aplikasi

#### b. Data Keuangan

- Data transaksi yang Anda input secara manual (nominal, kategori, tanggal, catatan)
- Data transaksi yang terdeteksi dari notifikasi perbankan (nominal, nama aplikasi sumber, waktu)
- Nama dan saldo dompet yang Anda daftarkan

#### c. Data Notifikasi

- Atur Keuangan membaca notifikasi dari aplikasi m-banking dan e-wallet yang Anda pilih untuk mendeteksi transaksi secara otomatis
- Kami **tidak** membaca notifikasi dari aplikasi lain (WhatsApp, SMS, email, dll.)
- Kami **tidak** menyimpan teks notifikasi mentah — hanya data transaksi yang berhasil diekstrak

- Versi aplikasi dan sistem operasi (untuk keperluan debug)
- ID anonim perangkat (untuk sinkronisasi data antar sesi)

This Privacy Policy explains how **Atur Keuangan**, developed by **SudutKode** ("we", "developer"), collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Atur Keuangan application ("App").

### 2. Data We Collect

#### a. Account Data

- A unique code (hash) derived from your Google email address — your actual email is never stored
- Display name you set within the App

#### b. Financial Data

- Transaction data you input manually (amount, category, date, notes)
- Transaction data detected from banking notifications (amount, source app name, time)
- Wallet names and balances you register

#### c. Notification Data

- Atur Keuangan reads notifications from m-banking and e-wallet apps you select, to automatically detect transactions
- We do **not** read notifications from other apps (WhatsApp, SMS, email, etc.)
- We do **not** store raw notification text — only successfully extracted transaction data

#### d. Technical Data

- App version and operating system (for debugging purposes)
- Anonymous device ID (for session data synchronization)

---

### 3. Purpose of Data Use

| Data              | Purpose                                        |
| ----------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| Account code      | Anonymously identify your account              |
| Transaction data  | Display history, reports, and financial charts |
| Notification data | Automatically record transactions              |
| Technical data    | Ensure the app functions correctly             |

We do **not** use your data for advertising, profiling, or any other commercial purpose.

---

### 4. Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored on our servers with encryption
- Data is only accessible through your connected Google account
- Your actual email is never stored in plain text (pseudonymization)
- We do **not sell** your data to any third party
- We do **not share** your data with third parties, unless required by law

---

### 5. Data Retention

- Active data is stored as long as your account is active
- After account deletion, all data will be permanently deleted from our servers within **30 days**
- Anonymous data used for internal statistics cannot be traced back to your identity

---

### 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** the data we store about you
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your account and all data via Settings → Logout / Delete Account
- **Withdraw consent** for notification access at any time via device Settings
- **Ask questions** about your data by contacting us via email above
